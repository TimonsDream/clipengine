Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

1. Overview

Movie Edits ("the App") is a personal content creation tool. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the App with TikTok's Content Posting API.

2. Information We Collect

The App collects only the minimum information required to function: a TikTok API access token, which is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to any third party. We do not collect names, email addresses, browsing data, or any other personal information.

3. How We Use Information

Your TikTok access token is used solely to publish video content to your TikTok account through TikTok's official Content Posting API. No other use is made of this token.

4. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. No data is stored on external servers. No data is transmitted to any third party other than TikTok's API servers for the purpose of posting content.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any user data with third parties. The only external communication is with TikTok's API servers to post content you have authorized.

6. Data Retention

Your TikTok access token is stored locally until you delete it or revoke access. Post history is maintained locally for your reference. You can delete all stored data at any time by removing the App's configuration files.

7. Your Rights

You can revoke the App's access to your TikTok account at any time through TikTok's account settings. You can delete all locally stored data at any time.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
