Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

1. Overview

ClipEngine ("the App", "we", "our") is a platform that enables indie artists and creators to produce and share short-form music video clips with cinematic visuals and synced lyrics. This Privacy Policy explains how ClipEngine handles information when you use our platform and its integration with TikTok's Content Posting API.

2. Information We Collect

When you use ClipEngine, we collect only the minimum information required to provide our services: a TikTok API access token obtained through TikTok's official OAuth authorization flow. This token is stored securely and is never transmitted to any unauthorized third party. ClipEngine does not collect names, email addresses, browsing data, or any other personal information beyond what is necessary for TikTok API authentication.

3. How We Use Information

ClipEngine uses your TikTok access token solely to publish video content to your TikTok account through TikTok's official Content Posting API on your behalf. No other use is made of this token.

4. Data Storage

All authentication data processed by ClipEngine is stored securely. No data is transmitted to any third party other than TikTok's API servers for the purpose of posting content that you have authorized through ClipEngine.

5. Data Sharing

ClipEngine does not sell, trade, or share any user data with third parties. The only external communication is with TikTok's API servers to post content you have authorized through the ClipEngine platform.

6. Data Retention

Your TikTok access token is retained by ClipEngine until you delete it or revoke access. You can delete all stored data at any time by removing your ClipEngine account or revoking access through TikTok's settings.

7. Your Rights

You can revoke ClipEngine's access to your TikTok account at any time through TikTok's account settings. You can request deletion of all data associated with your use of ClipEngine at any time.

8. Children's Privacy

ClipEngine is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

ClipEngine may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
